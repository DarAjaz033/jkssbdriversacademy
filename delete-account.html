Steps to request deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to info@jkssbdriversacademy.co.in with the subject line "Account Deletion Request - Drivers Academy".

In your email, please include the registered phone number associated with your account.

You will receive a confirmation within 48 hours once your request is being processed.

Data that gets deleted

  • Phone number and authentication credentials
  • User profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Account info and app activity history
  • Progress data and mock test scores
  • All personal data collected during app usage

Data that may be retained

We may retain certain data strictly as required by law for financial auditing, tax purposes, or legal compliance.

Retention period

  • Permanent Deletion: All eligible data will be permanently deleted from our primary databases within 7 days of a confirmed request.
  • Backup Purge: Residual data in encrypted system backups will be fully purged within 30 days.
